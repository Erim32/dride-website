What & Why

We’re a bunch of car fanatics who believe that the vehicles we are driving are unnecessarily primitive and downright dangerous! We’ve designed Dride to change that, and with it, the way we drive. Just as the time when no-one used seat belts rapidly faded we believe that the era of unintelligent cars should be a distant memory, and we are here to bring it about sooner than anyone expects.

INDICATOR RING

While driving you cant interact with screens or small icons, The interaction have to be simpler, That why we've created the indicator ring, A simple and intuitive way to get information without getting distracted.

FRONT INDICATOR

The front indicator is soft light placed in the front of your car aimed to interact with the environment, Blink when you are approaching too fast, Alert when you forgot a child in the car.

Keeping you safe. Always.
